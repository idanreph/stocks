What this scanner does differently

Standard screeners give you ratios. This scanner gives you a structural state diagnosis. Every number is computed from exact Yahoo Finance rows — deterministic, traceable, explainable.

Layer 1

6-block scoring engine

Revenue & margin quality, solvency, liquidity, earnings quality, cash engine, and capital allocation — each as a deterministic rule with exact thresholds.

Layer 2

DST vector mapping

Every financial finding classified as σ (stress), κ_a / κ_c / κ_i (masking type), or ρ (healing). Not all masking is the same — three types, three interventions.

Layer 3

Trajectory + time-to-failure

Θ computed across 4 periods. dΘ/dt shows direction. At current slope, how many periods until each regime threshold breaks — or how far away you are from it.

Income Statement tells a story → Cash Flow + Balance Sheet proves or contradicts it → A(t) vs Θ(t) = observability gap

κ is not scalar — it is a vector

Not all masking is neglect. The scanner classifies every masking finding into one of three types. The difference is whether it is structural, forced, or conscious.

κ_a
Accumulated · FIX
Past choices built hidden fragility
Goodwill buildup from acquisitions. Earnings consistently above cash flow. Years of dilution. AR growing faster than revenue. Grows with neglect. Penalized fully.
κ_c
Conscripted · MITIGATE
Domain or sector forces it
Consumer brand negative working capital (collect before paying suppliers — Apple, Walmart). Commodity shock on margins. Rate regime repricing debt. Cannot be eliminated. Penalty capped at floor rate.
κ_i
Intentional · ACCEPT
Documented tradeoff with a rationale
Low-rate debt funding high-ROIC buybacks. Intentional FCF burn during expansion. Restructuring charge to clear future costs. Accepted — but monitored. Becomes κ_a when the rationale expires.
